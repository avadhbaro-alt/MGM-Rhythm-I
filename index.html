A Prestigious Beginning

The MGM Rhythm Academy was inaugurated to create a musical stress-free environment for the students and staff of mgm campus The MGM Rhythm Institute was inaugurated by the auspicious hands of:

  • Padmashree, Bhavgandharv Pandit Hridaynath ji Mangeshkar
  • Hon'ble Chairman and Trustee Kamal Kishore Ji Kadam Sir
  • Hon'ble Trustee & Medical Director MGMIHS Dr. Sudhir Kadam Sir
  • Hon'ble Pro-Vice-Chancellor MGMIHS Dr. Shashank Dalvi Sir
  • Hon'ble Dr. Nitin Kadam Sir
  • Registrar MGMIHS Dr. Rajesh Goel Sir
  • Dean Dr. Narshetty Sir
  • Director of MGM Rhythm Institute Mr. Pramod Sarkate
  • Assoc. Director of MGM Rhythm InstituteSwaraj Sarkate
